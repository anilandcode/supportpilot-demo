# Billing and Account Management

## Payment methods

AcmeDesk accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, and ACH bank transfers for annual Business and Enterprise contracts. Invoices are issued monthly or annually depending on the billing cycle. All prices are in USD.

## Annual discount

Annual billing saves 20% on Pro and Business. Pro becomes $9.60 per user per month, and Business becomes $19.20 per user per month. Annual plans are billed upfront for the full 12-month term.

## Upgrades and downgrades

When a workspace upgrades mid-cycle, AcmeDesk prorates the charge so the customer pays only for the remaining days at the new plan rate. Adding seats mid-cycle uses the same proration logic. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

## Refund policy

First-time upgrades from Free to a paid plan are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of the initial charge. Renewals, seat additions, and later plan upgrades are not eligible for refunds. Customers can request a refund by emailing billing@acmedesk.example with the workspace name and charge date.

## Cancellation

Customers can cancel from Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. The workspace moves to the Free plan, and existing data, issues, and project history are retained.
