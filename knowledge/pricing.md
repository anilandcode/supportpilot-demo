# Pricing Plans

AcmeDesk offers four plans for product and support teams.

## Free

The Free plan costs $0 forever. It supports up to 5 members per workspace, unlimited issues, unlimited projects, GitHub and Slack integrations, and 250 MB of workspace storage. It does not include cycles, roadmaps, advanced analytics, or priority support.

## Pro

The Pro plan costs $12 per user per month. Annual billing saves 20%, bringing the effective price to $9.60 per user per month. Pro includes unlimited members, unlimited uploads up to 10 GB per workspace, cycles, roadmaps, advanced filtering, and priority support with a 24-hour response SLA.

## Business

The Business plan costs $24 per user per month. Annual billing saves 20%, bringing the effective price to $19.20 per user per month. Business adds SAML SSO, role-based access control, custom roles, audit logs with 90-day retention, advanced analytics, a 4-hour support SLA, and a dedicated customer success manager for teams with more than 20 seats.

## Enterprise

Enterprise is custom priced for organizations with 100 or more users or strict security requirements. Enterprise includes custom data residency in the US or EU, 365-day audit logs, 1-hour priority support, custom onboarding and training, and a named account manager.
